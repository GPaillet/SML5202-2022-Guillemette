What are the different regions in France?

This map represents all of France's departements as well as the outlining of France's regions. There are 100 departements for 13 regions (18 if you count territories outside of France):

  • Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes
  • Bourgogne-Franche-Comté
  • Bretagne
  • Centre-Val de Loire
  • Grand Est
  • Hauts-de-France
  • Ile-de-France
  • Normandie
  • Nouvelle-Acquitaine
  • Occitanie
  • Pays de la Loire
  • Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur
