Roses des sables

Roses des sables are a french dessert made out of chocolate and cereals.

They are really simple and not really time-consuming

Ingredients

  • 150g black chocolate
  • 100g butter or margarine
  • 100g corn flakes

Instructions

  1. Break the chocolate and make it malt with the butter
  2. Add some sugar (according to your own tastes), put the cornflakes in a bowl then pour the chocolate on it
  3. Mix it well and then make little heaps on a baking paper or aluminum foil
  4. Put it in the fridge for a few hours or overnight
  5. You can add some icing suger before eating
