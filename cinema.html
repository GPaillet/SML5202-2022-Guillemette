French people and cinema

Did you know the first moving picture was actually made by French brothers? In 1895, les frères Lumières presented their own machine called a Cinématographe to make their own little movies

Nowadays, French movie theaters actually have the highest amount of people coming to see movies in the whole Europe and most movies are partly financed by state money (through the CNC)
