How to use the imperfect

When do you use the "imparfait de l'indicatif"?

There are many situations where you use the imperfect tense, you use it for: recurrent actions , describing feelings, describing age and time, general descriptions in the past or even ongoing actions puncuated by another action

Steps to conjugate a verb in the French imperfect tense

  1. Take the first plural form of the verb in the present tense
  2. Remove -ons ending
  3. Add the appropriate ending
