Be aware: In French, all nouns have a gender. Either feminine or masculine. The noun's gender influence the determinant: you will use either le or un for masculine nouns and either la or une for feminine nouns.

You can also find the determinant " l' ", this determinant is only used when the noun begins with a vowel

CAREFUL!!! Some nouns can be either feminine or masculine, learn the gender with the noun!
